Run three passes on the draft, in order. After each pass, summarize what changed and ask the user: **"Continue to next pass?"** before proceeding.

## Pass 1 — Architectural Structure
Deconstruct the macro-layout to optimize content flow and reader retention.
- **Blueprint Audit**: Verify a compelling introduction, a logical body, and a definitive conclusion.
- **Density Control**: If the body exceeds 5 sections, consolidate ideas to prevent reader fatigue.
- **Resolution**: Draft a strong, resonant conclusion if the current one is weak or missing.

## Pass 2 — Mechanics & Clarity
Micro-edit the prose to maximize readability and strip away friction.
- **Voice Shift**: Convert passive phrasing into active, high-energy verbs.
- **Pacing Control**: Split sentences exceeding 25 words to improve reading rhythm.
- **Cognitive Load**: Swap out heavy industry jargon for accessible, universal language.

## Pass 3 — The Hook & Retention
Optimize the entry point to instantly capture attention and lower bounce rates.
- **First-Line Audit**: Analyze the opening sentence to ensure it immediately earns the reader's time.
- **Angles**: Propose 3 distinct alternative opening hooks if the original lacks punch.
- **Selection**: Present the options clearly and let the user select the final winner.
