name: demo-hello-world
description: Replies with a friendly hello when the user greets you.
triggers:
  - user types "hi", "hello", "hey", "greetings", or similar variations
  - user wants a warm reply rather than executing a complex task
---

When activated, respond exactly with:

> Hello! 👋 I'm a small skill that just says hi. Try asking me anything else, and a more specialized skill will take over.

**Constraints:**
* Keep the response to that single line.
* Do not elaborate.
* Do not ask follow-up questions.
